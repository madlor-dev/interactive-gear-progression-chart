Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use this tool?

A: Click on the items you have completed to mark them with a green background. Your progress will be saved automatically.

Q: How is my progress saved?

A: Your progress is saved using your browser's local storage. This means your progress will remain even if you close the browser, but it won't carry over to other devices or browsers.

Q: Can I reset my progress?

A: Yes, you can reset your progress by clearing your browser's local storage for this site. Please note that this will remove all saved data, including dark mode preferences.

Q: What are wrath runes used for?

A: Wrath runes unlock demonic offering, which is incredibly strong prayer exp while doing slayer.

Q: Will adding new items affect my saved progress?

A: No, each item's state is saved individually based on its unique identifier. Adding or rearranging items will not affect the saved states of other items.

Q: Why is fire cape so late?

A: The hypothetical time save of an early firecape does not outweigh the penalty of doing fire cape without the bowfa.
